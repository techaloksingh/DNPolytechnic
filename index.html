D.N Polytechnic Meerut is one of the oldest and renowned polytechnic of Uttar Pradesh. This institute was established in 1956 by Dev Nagri Society. In 1962 after the "Memorandum of Understanding" with state government, a dedicated society named "D.N. Polytechnic Society, Meerut" was formed by Government of UP. It is 100% aided by the government of Uttar Pradesh. Currently this Institute is offering five courses – Diploma in Civil Engineering, Diploma in Mechanical Engineering, Diploma in Electrical Engineering, Diploma in Computer Science & Engineering and Post graduate Diploma in Bio- technology. All the courses are approved by AICTE New Delhi. All the admissions are done through Joint Entrance Examination Council Uttar Pradesh (JEECUP).
The Institute is continuously serving the society for last 65 year by providing technical man power .This year 2021 is the “Sapphire Jubilee” year of the Institute, well established alumni is sufficient to validate this claim of ours.
